Creamy Pasta Sauce

So rich!

Ingredients

  • 4 shallots finely chopped
  • 4 bacon slices rind removed and chopped
  • 200g button mushrooms sliced
  • 250ml cream for cooking softened

Steps

  1. Combine shallots and bacon in large frying pan over a moderate heat and cook, stirring until the bacon is crisp.
  2. Add mushrooms and stir until softened.
  3. Add Cream for cooking and simmer for 5 to 7 minutes allowing the flavours to meld.
  4. Season to taste.
